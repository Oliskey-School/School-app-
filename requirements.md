# Extended School App Features

## Core Administration

* Multi-school / multi-tenant support
* Role-based access control (Owner, Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent)
* Fine-grained permissions per module
* Audit logs for all critical actions
* Organization-wide settings & branding

## Student Management

* Student lifecycle (admission → graduation → alumni)
* Class, arm, house, and stream management
* Attendance (daily, period-based, biometric-ready)
* Behavior & discipline tracking
* Health records & emergency contacts

## Teacher & Staff Management

* Staff profiles & qualifications
* Timetable & workload management
* Leave & substitute handling
* Performance reviews
* Payroll-ready data export

## Academic Management

* Subject & curriculum builder
* Continuous assessment & exams
* Auto-grading + manual overrides
* GPA / CGPA calculation rules
* Report cards (PDF + portal view)

## Learning & LMS

* Assignment creation & submissions
* Online tests & quizzes
* Lesson plans & syllabus tracking
* File, video, and link resources
* Progress analytics per student

## Parent Portal

* Real-time academic progress
* Attendance & behavior alerts
* Fee status & receipts
* Messaging with teachers/admin
* Multiple children under one account

## Communication

* In-app messaging
* Email & SMS notifications
* Push notifications (mobile)
* Announcement boards
* Emergency broadcast mode

## Finance & Billing

* Fee structure & installments
* Online payments (local gateways)
* Automatic invoices & receipts
* Discounts, scholarships, fines
* Financial reports & exports

## Scheduling & Timetable

* Smart timetable generator
* Clash detection
* Room & lab allocation
* Exam scheduling
* Calendar sync (Google/Outlook)

## Library Management

* Book catalog & ISBN support
* Borrow / return tracking
* Late fee automation
* Digital library links
* Inventory reports

## Transport Management

* Bus & route management
* Student–bus assignment
* Pickup/drop attendance
* GPS-ready integration
* Transport fee handling

## Hostel / Boarding

* Room & bed allocation
* Hostel attendance
* Visitor logs
* Mess / meal tracking
* Hostel fee management

## Analytics & Reporting

* Academic performance dashboards
* Attendance trends
* Teacher effectiveness metrics
* Financial analytics
* Custom report builder

## Security & Compliance

* Data encryption (at rest & transit)
* Two-factor authentication
* Session & device management
* Backup & disaster recovery
* GDPR / local compliance ready

## Integrations

* Payment gateways
* SMS / Email providers
* Government education systems
* HR & accounting software
* API & Webhooks

## Mobile & Offline

* Mobile-first responsive UI
* Android / iOS apps
* Offline data capture
* Auto-sync on reconnect

## Customization & Extensibility

* Module enable/disable per school
* Custom fields & forms
* White-label support
* Plugin system
* Feature flags

## AI-Powered Features

* Performance prediction

* Auto timetable optimization
